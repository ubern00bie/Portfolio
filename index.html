About Me

Hi, I'm Jordan.
It's a pleasure to meet you.

I took an interest in computers at an early age, holding a flashlight for my dad as he worked. Throughout middle school I learned the basics of web development/programming through sites like MySpace and playing with the code in video games in order to create mods of my own. I took an unconventional detour during college leading to a bachelor’s in an unrelated field. While I was successful, I didn’t enjoy what I was doing. I recently moved back to Kansas City and am now a student of the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp through the University of Kansas. I’m passionate about learning, ambitious and have an attention to detail with a solid work ethic.
