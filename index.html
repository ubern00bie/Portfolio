About Me

Hi, I'm Jordan.
It's a pleasure to meet you.

I took an interest in computers at an early age, while I held a flashlight for my father as he worked. I moved back to Kansas City and am now a student of the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp through the University of Kansas. I’m passionate about learning, ambitious and have an attention to detail with a solid work ethic.
